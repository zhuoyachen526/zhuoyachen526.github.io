Trace

Trace is a iOS game. It requires players to trace the line and gain as much points as possible

The purpose of this game is to stay on track for as long as possible. Players have to keep their fingers on the white line without falling out for the whole time and go through all the nodes sequentially.

Check out the app here on Devpost.

Android Calculator

This calculator was a simple calculator app for the Android platform. It can perform addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division on two numbers. I implemented it used the Model-View-Controller (MVC) architectural pattern for a school project.
